About US

The Techfest College Ambassador Program is a pan India networking program that aims to create a network of like-minded and tech-savvy students across India. We aim to develop and nurture future leaders in the field of tech who shall act as Techfest’s representatives, brand evangelists and promoters in their respective institutions.
